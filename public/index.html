Our Story

More Than a Round of Golf

Founded in December 2025, The Fairway Society was born from a simple idea: the golf course is the ultimate meeting ground. What started as a small group of friends sharing a love of the game has grown into a thriving community of professionals who understand that the best business conversations happen on the fairway.

We bring together ambitious individuals from all walks of life, united by a passion for golf and a desire to build meaningful connections. Whether you're a seasoned player or just picking up the clubs, you'll find a welcoming community ready to tee off together.

Why Join

The Fairway Advantage

More than just golf. It's a community built on shared passion and mutual growth.

Network on the Green

Connect with professionals from diverse industries in a relaxed setting. Some of the best partnerships have been forged between the first tee and the 18th hole.

Regular Events

From weekly rounds to major tournaments, we host events throughout the year at premier courses. There's always a game to join and new people to meet.

All Skill Levels

Whether you're a single-digit handicapper or just discovering the sport, our society welcomes players at every level. It's about the camaraderie, not the scorecard.

Premier Courses

Enjoy access to a curated selection of top-tier courses. We negotiate group rates and exclusive tee times so you can play the best courses at the best value.

Social Events

Golf is just the beginning. From post-round dinners to annual galas, our social calendar keeps the connections growing well beyond the course.

Golf Trips

Join our members on curated golf getaways to iconic destinations. From domestic escapes to international tours, adventure awaits beyond your home course.
